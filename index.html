ABOUT

Aklanon Speech to Text transcriber is a free and realtime website using voice recognition to transcribe from audio recordings to readable text. It is an accessible site that welcomes all users who wants to know Aklanon words. However, only limited aklanon words can offer of this website.

To begin with, first click the start button for recording and as soon as the user taps the copy button after recording, words will be display.
It has an hassle free features that easy to use because of its simpleness and a user friendly visuals to guide the user to its next step.

It contains most important pages, the homepage which is the website's primary focuses, as well as the pages for about us and contact us for aklanon speech to text transcriber informations.
